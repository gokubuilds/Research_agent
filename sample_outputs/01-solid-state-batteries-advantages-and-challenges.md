# Advantages of Solid-State Batteries
Solid-state batteries offer numerous benefits, including:

* Higher energy density and improved safety
* Ability to use metallic lithium for the anode and oxides or sulfides for the cathode, enhancing energy density
* Solid-state lithium-ion batteries are a promising alternative to traditional lithium-ion batteries
* NASA has developed solid-state battery technology with twice the energy density of lithium-ion batteries

# Challenges and Limitations
Despite the potential benefits, solid-state batteries face several challenges and limitations, including:

* High cost
* Mechanical and interfacial instability
* Dendrite formation
* The technology is still relatively new, and the cost is much greater than traditional batteries
