# Benefits of AI in Healthcare
### Improved Diagnostic Capabilities
* AI can help diagnose breast cancer from mammogram images, leading to faster lifesaving treatment and reducing costs for hospitals.

* AI has superior capabilities in diagnostic accuracy, or diagnosing and detecting diseases early, from technologies such as X-rays, mammograms, ultrasounds, or CT scans.
* AI can analyze complex medical issues, such as diagnosing cancer or Alzheimer’s disease from images.
* AI can conduct and improve a broad range of healthcare functions such as clinical decision support, responding to patient queries, detection and diagnosis of conditions, designing and prescribing drugs, monitoring patients with analysis of vital signs, (in acute care settings and remotely), performing procedures, improving analyses of tissue in pathology studies, analyzing doctor-patient interactions and conversations and improving analyzing radiology (and other specialties) films and studies.
