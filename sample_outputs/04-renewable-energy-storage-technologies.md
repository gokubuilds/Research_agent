# Energy Storage Basics
### Key Concepts and Principles

* Energy storage is the capture of energy produced at one time for use at a later time to reduce imbalances between energy demand and energy production.
* Energy storage devices are “charged” when they absorb energy, either directly from renewable generation devices or indirectly from the electricity grid.
* Energy storage systems allow businesses to save energy from the grid or on-site renewables and then use it as needed.

# Applications of Energy Storage
### Improving Power System Reliability

* A variety of energy storage systems can be used to help improve power system reliability by balancing utility grids and electricity distribution or smoothing the integration of renewable energy from sun, wind, and hydro power.
